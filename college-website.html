About Our College

A Legacy of Learning, A Future of Leaders

Founded in 1998, Belal Model College began with a simple but powerful vision: to provide world-class education accessible to every aspiring student. From humble beginnings with 200 students and 12 faculty members, we have grown into one of the region's most respected educational institutions.

Today, we serve over 3,500 students across multiple programs, guided by 120+ dedicated faculty members who are experts in their fields.

  • Consistently ranked in the top 10 colleges nationally
  • 97.8% HSC pass rate for the class of 2023
  • Over 12,000 proud alumni across the world
  • NAAC A+ accredited institution

Our mission is to cultivate a dynamic learning environment that fosters intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical leadership. We are committed to academic rigour, inclusive education, and producing graduates who contribute meaningfully to society.

  • Deliver quality education with modern pedagogy
  • Build character alongside academic knowledge
  • Foster research and innovation culture
  • Ensure inclusive access regardless of background

We envision Belal Model College as a globally recognised centre of learning where students are empowered to become visionary leaders, compassionate citizens, and lifelong learners — equipped to tackle tomorrow's greatest challenges with confidence and integrity.

  • Become a globally ranked educational institution
  • Lead in research and technological innovation
  • Build a network of impactful global alumni
  • Champion sustainable, equitable education
